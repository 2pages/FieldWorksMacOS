Translation Editor Demos

FieldWorks Translation Editor provides tools for editing and checking Scripture translations.

Here are brief demonstrations of procedures to perform typical tasks. The first slide of a demo displays the main concept, process, or result (for example, FieldWorks projects, backing up projects, or styles applied to text).

Click a hyperlink to see and read a visual demo. You click to go to each step at your own pace.
